Yet, as we navigate the complexities of the 21st century, the challenges facing the global education system are multifarious and daunting. Equally crucial is the recognition that education is not a one-size-fits-all endeavor. Only through such an inclusive and culturally responsive approach can we truly ensure that no child is left behind.
