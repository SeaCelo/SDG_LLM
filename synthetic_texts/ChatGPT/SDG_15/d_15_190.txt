The Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 15, also known as "Life on Land," is one of the seventeen SDGs adopted by the United Nations in 2015. This goal focuses on the preservation, restoration, and sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, aiming to secure the future of vital resources and ensuring life thrives on land.
