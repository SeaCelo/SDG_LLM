It embodies a comprehensive vision for creating resilient and sustainable economies and societies, while addressing the need for global partnerships that promote these goals.

Secondly, while industrialization has contributed to economic growth, it has also placed undue strain on natural resources, causing environmental degradation and increased carbon emissions.
